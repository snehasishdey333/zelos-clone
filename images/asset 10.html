An easy app for on-demand staffing

Dispatch tasks and shifts

Upload your tasks and shifts. Then assign your team members or watch them self-signup!

Self-signup allows team members to choose the schedule and assignments that work best for them. This is a great way to distribute a schedule (or ad-hoc tasks) quickly while accommodating the needs and preferences of team members.

GDPR compliant database

If your project involves companies or individuals in the EU, it is required that your team software is GDPR compliant and gives your team members control over their own data.

Zelos allows all app users to request access and changes to their data, and is compliant with all data protection and privacy requirements of The General Data Protection Regulation.

Chats and announcements

Effective communication is a critical component of successful team management. Zelos lets you set up various chatrooms with your team members – either in groups, 1:1, or dynamically based on who’s working today.

You can also keep your team members informed about important updates, changes, and developments with the easy announcement feature. Ensure that everyone is on the same page (and make sure everyone has actually read the latest newsletter).
